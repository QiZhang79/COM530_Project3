This is Me. @QiZhang

Hi, my name is Qi Zhang. I'm a currently a graduate student majored in Information Architecture. And I hope to be a web designer after I graduate.

I studied Advertising during my undergraduate period. Although I was not an engineer or science background student, my interdisciplinary background still helps me a lot. For instance, my undergraduate major experience helped me to develop a sense of choosing attractive things, which pushed me to keep high standards for my own web design.

I have two important turning points for my career goal. The first one happened in my third year in university. My friend asked me whether I was interested to join a mobile app development team, and I could do UE and Product Management work. I feel lucky right now since I choose to accept the invitation at that moment which helped me to get in touch with those web/software things. So with the experience in that team, I became a real product manager after I graduated and my first work lasted one year.

It's near Chicago River. I love Chicago's Night.

The one-year product manager work was not that cheerful. My boss pushed me a lot and I always stayed up late without extra pay. And my PM team had a bad relationship with developer team which made me disappointed. I thought to study aboard and learning what I didn't know is a challenge/opportunity. So I made my mind from the end of 2015.

Around this time last year, I didn't have a very clear career goal. But one day, I browsed my friend's own website and he showed me lots of tricks. I suddenly these found attractive things were so interesting. And combining with the current market, I thought why not to be a web designer. So I chose to register web design relative classes and self-studied.

This summer break, I had an internship in a local start up(Pyroweb.net) working as front-end designer. Every time I finished the assignments, I felt very satisfied. This experience really made me affirm my choice for sure.
